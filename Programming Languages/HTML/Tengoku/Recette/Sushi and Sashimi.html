Sushi and Sashimi

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • fish (salmon, tuna, etc.)
  • soy sauce
  • wasabi
  • seaweed sheets

Instructions

  1. Cook sushi rice, season it with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt
  2. For sushi, form the rice into small mounds and top with slices of raw fish
