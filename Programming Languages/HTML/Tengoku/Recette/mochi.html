Mochi

Ingredients

  • Mochiko (sweet rice flour)
  • water
  • sugar
  • filling (red bean paste, ice cream)

Instructions

  1. Mix mochiko, water, and sugar, then steam or microwave until it becomes a sticky dough
  2. Shape into small balls and fill with red bean paste or ice cream
  3. Dust with cornstarch to prevent sticking
