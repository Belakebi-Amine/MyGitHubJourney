Teriyaki

Ingredients

  • Chicken, beef, or salmon
  • soy sauce
  • mirin
  • sugar
  • garlic
  • ginger

Instructions

  1. Mix soy sauce, mirin, sugar, garlic, and ginger to make the teriyaki sauce
  2. Grill or pan-fry the meat or fish, basting with the sauce until caramelized
  3. Serve with steamed rice and vegetables
