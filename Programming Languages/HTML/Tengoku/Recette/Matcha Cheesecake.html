Matcha Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheesecake
  • sugar
  • eggs
  • matcha powder
  • graham cracker crust

Instructions

  1. Blend cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and matcha powder
  2. Pour the mixture over a graham cracker crust
  3. Bake until set, then chill before serving
