Gyoza

Ingredients

  • Gyoza wrappers
  • ground pork
  • garlic
  • soy sauce
  • sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Mix ground pork, finely chopped cabbage, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and sesame oil
  2. Place a small amount of filling in the center of a gyoza wrapper, fold, and seal the edges
  3. Pan-fry until the bottom is crispy, then add a bit of water, cover, and steam until cooked through
