Sushi and Sashimi

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced beef
  • tofu
  • shiitake mushrooms
  • napa cabbage
  • soy sauce
  • mirin
  • sugar

Instructions

  1. In a pot, combine soy sauce, mirin, and sugar to create the base broth
  2. Add sliced beef, tofu, mushrooms, and cabbage, and simmer until everything is cooked
  3. Serve hot, sometimes with a raw egg for dipping
