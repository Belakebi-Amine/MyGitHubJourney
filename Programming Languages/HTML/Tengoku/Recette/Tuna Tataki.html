Tuna Tataki

Ingredients

  • Tuna steak
  • fsoy sauce
  • mirin
  • rice vinegar
  • garlic
  • ginger

Instructions

  1. Sear the tuna steak briefly on all sides in a hot pan
  2. Mix soy sauce, mirin, rice vinegar, garlic, and ginger to make the sauce
  3. Slice the tuna thinly and drizzle with the sauce. Garnish with green onions
