Ramen

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodle
  • pork or chicken
  • broth (miso, soy, or pork)
  • nori
  • green onions
  • boiled egg

Instructions

  1. Prepare broth by simmering bones or using pre-made miso or soy base
  2. Cook ramen noodles in boiling water
  3. Serve the noodles in the broth, topped with meat, boiled egg, nori, and green onions
