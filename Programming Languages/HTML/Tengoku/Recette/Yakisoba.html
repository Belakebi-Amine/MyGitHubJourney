Yakisoba

Ingredients

  • Yakisoba noodles
  • pork or chicken
  • cabbage
  • carrots
  • yakisoba sauce

Instructions

  1. Stir-fry pork or chicken in a hot pan with vegetables like cabbage and carrots
  2. Add yakisoba noodles and yakisoba sauce, and stir until everything is well mixed and heated through
