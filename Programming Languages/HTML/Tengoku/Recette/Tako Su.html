Tako Su

Ingredients

  • Cooked octopus
  • rice vinegar/li>
  • soy sauce
  • sugar
  • mirin

Instructions

  1. Slice octopus thinly and slice cucumber into rounds
  2. Mix rice vinegar, soy sauce, sugar, and mirin to make the dressing
  3. Toss the octopus and cucumber in the dressing and serve chilled
