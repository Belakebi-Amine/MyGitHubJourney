Daifuku

Ingredients

  • mochiko
  • water
  • sugar
  • filling (matcha, chocolate, fruit)

Instructions

  1. Prepare mochi dough by steaming or microwaving mochiko, water, and sugar
  2. Flatten the dough and wrap it around a sweet filling like matcha, chocolate, or fruit
  3. Dust with cornstarch to prevent sticking
