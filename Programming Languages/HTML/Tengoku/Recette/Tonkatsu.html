Tonkatsu

Ingredients

  • Pork cutlet
  • flour
  • eggs
  • panko breadcrumbs
  • oil
  • tonkatsu sauce

Instructions

  1. Coat the pork cutlet in flour, dip in beaten eggs, then coat with panko breadcrumbs
  2. Fry the cutlet in hot oil until golden brown and crispy
  3. Serve with shredded cabbage, rice, and tonkatsu sauce
