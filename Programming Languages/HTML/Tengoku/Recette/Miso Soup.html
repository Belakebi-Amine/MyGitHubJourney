Miso Soup

Ingredients

  • Miso paste
  • tofu
  • seaweed
  • green onions
  • dashi stock

Instructions

  1. Heat dashi stock and dissolve miso paste in it
  2. Add diced tofu and seaweed, and cook for 2-3 minutes
  3. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot
